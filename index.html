FAQ

Questions & Answers

What is Oneirophis?+

Oneirophis transforms dream descriptions into cinematic AI-generated visual experiences. You describe a dream, and AI reconstructs it as a short cinematic video. It's a visual dream diary with a personal archive.

How does dream reconstruction work?+

You record or write a description of your dream. Our AI analyzes the narrative — extracting scenes, characters, mood, and atmosphere — then generates a cinematic visual reconstruction. The process takes about 60 seconds of input.

Is this dream interpretation?+

No. Oneirophis does not interpret, analyze, or explain your dreams. We don't claim psychological insight or hidden meanings. We visualize them — we turn your dream descriptions into cinematic visual experiences.
